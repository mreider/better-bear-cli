What Better Bear does

Better Bear provides a CLI and MCP server that accesses your Bear notes via Apple's CloudKit API. It runs locally on your macOS computer and communicates only with Apple's servers to read and write your notes.

Your data

  • Better Bear accesses your Bear notes using your own iCloud credentials.
  • All data processing happens locally on your machine.
  • No data is collected, stored, or transmitted to the developer or any third party.
  • See the Privacy Policy for full details.

Data processing

Better Bear does not process personal data on behalf of users. It is a local tool that acts as a client to Apple CloudKit using your own credentials. There is no server-side data processing, no data storage by the developer, and no sub-processors.

When used with Claude Desktop or Claude Code, note content you share in conversations is processed by Anthropic under their own privacy policy and terms of service.
